Latest News

Goku story is not over yet! Theres a whole new saga waiting to unfold in Dragon Ball DAIMA, premiering TOMORROW! In addition to releasing his new video game Sparking Zero!!. This anime is considered a last gift from Akira Toriyama, the creator of the franchise, who worked on the project before his death. Although his legacy is now indelible, there is a detail in this new anime that has convinced fans that his spirit is still alive in the series.





One Piece anime on hiatus until April 2025, the news has paralyzed fans of the anime, as this will be the longest hiatus since the beginning of its broadcast in 1999. The adaptation, which is currently adapting the Egghead arc, will go on hiatus after episode 1,122 for, according to the words of the presenters, "recharge the batteries", and when it returns in 2025 it will do so in a new schedule on Japanese television.
